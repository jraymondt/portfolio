A long time ago, not that far away . . .



Episode I: The Phantom Jimbob


I was born in Perth, Western Australia in 1979. I grew up in the suburbs of Perth and spent my formative working years at the Windsor Hotel in South Perth.
I was a cook, I learned the ways of the kitchen and learned to work with my hands, to create things with my mind.
I honed some very rudimentary crafting skills in HTML using frontpage98 in the late 90's and early 2000s.
In my naivety I thought I could make a website and get paid for it. So designed, photographed and knocked together a website for the Windsor Hotel.



Episode II: Hospitality of the Clones


I spent the next 20 years in hospitality, working in some of the best hotels in Perth, including Liar's Saloon, JoeBlacks and the BelEyre Tavern.
If you are wondering why none of these places exist anymore, it's because they crumbled into fine cheese particles after they were destroyed by the Galactic Empire.

Episode IV: A New Hope


I was a courier; I leaned into driving and delivering packages to rebel scum. I re-ignited my passion for web development and have been waiting for the right time to return to it.
